Introduction

Welcome to ProtectQR Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using our App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

What You Provide

  • QR Code Content: Text, URLs, or data you input to generate QR codes. This is processed locally on your device only.
  • Passwords: If you encrypt QR codes, passwords are stored locally on your device only.

What We Collect Automatically

  • App Settings: Your preferences (theme, font, vibration, sound settings) stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device info needed for app functionality.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Payment information
  • QR code content (processed locally only)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide QR code generation, scanning, and encryption features
  • To remember your app preferences
  • To maintain and improve the app

Data Storage and Security

  • All QR code data and passwords are stored locally on your device only
  • We do not have servers that store your QR code content
  • Encryption happens locally on your device
  • Your data is never transmitted to our servers

Permissions

Camera

Used only to scan QR codes. Camera feed is not recorded or stored.

Storage/Photos

Used only to save QR code images you generate. We do not read your existing photos.

Vibration

Used for haptic feedback when scanning (optional, can be disabled).

Third-Party Services

Used for font options. No personal information is transmitted.

Payment processing is handled by Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not see your payment information.

Firebase

May collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal information. We may share data only if required by law or with trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Reset app preferences through settings
  • Revoke permissions through device settings
  • Disable features like vibration and sound

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy
