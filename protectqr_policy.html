Welcome to ProtectQR Pro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Important: ProtectQR Pro is designed with privacy and security as our top priorities. All encryption and decryption processes occur locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your encrypted QR codes, passwords, or decrypted content to any external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

ProtectQR Pro allows you to create encrypted QR codes by entering text messages and passwords. This information is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect certain information automatically when you use the app, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature usage, and crash reports (if enabled)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and mobile network information

1.3 Camera and Storage Permissions

ProtectQR Pro requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera: Used to scan QR codes. Camera access is only active when you use the scanning feature.
  • Storage/Gallery: Used to save generated QR codes to your device's gallery and to scan QR codes from images.

All QR code scanning and generation occurs locally on your device. We do not access, store, or transmit any images or QR code data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the ProtectQR Pro service
  • To process your requests and transactions
  • To send you technical notices and support messages
  • To respond to your comments and questions
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All QR codes, passwords, and encrypted data are stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect your data when creating encrypted QR codes. Your passwords are never stored in plain text and are only used for encryption/decryption processes.

3.2 App Preferences

ProtectQR Pro may store app preferences (such as theme settings) locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system.

3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • All encryption operations use AES-256 encryption standard
  • Data processing occurs entirely on your device
  • No sensitive data is transmitted over the network
  • Regular security updates and patches

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information when you explicitly consent to such sharing
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities
  • Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies to facilitate our service (e.g., analytics, crash reporting). These providers have access to limited, anonymized data only

  • Opt-Out: You can disable analytics and crash reporting in app settings (if available)
  • Permissions: You can revoke camera and storage permissions at any time through your device settings

7. Children's Privacy
