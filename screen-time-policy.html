Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

ScreenTime ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 App Usage Data

ScreenTime collects information about your device usage to provide screen time tracking and analytics features:

  • App Usage Statistics: We collect data about which apps you use, how long you use them, and when you use them. This data is collected through Android's UsageStatsManager API.
  • Usage Patterns: We analyze your usage patterns to provide insights, trends, and recommendations.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device model and Android version may be collected for app functionality.

1.2 User Preferences

We store your app preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • Accent color choices
  • Font preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Goals and app limits you set

1.3 Permissions

ScreenTime requires the following permissions to function:

  • Usage Access Permission: Required to track app usage time and provide accurate statistics. This permission allows us to see which apps you use and for how long, but we cannot access the content within those apps.
  • Accessibility Service (Optional): If you choose to use app blocking features, this permission is required to detect when blocked apps are opened. We do not record any sensitive information, passwords, or input data.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Track and display your screen time and app usage statistics
  • Provide daily, weekly, and monthly analytics
  • Generate usage insights and trends
  • Enable goal setting and progress tracking
  • Manage app limits and restrictions (if enabled)

2.2 Personalization

  • Customize the app appearance based on your preferences
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Send notifications based on your preferences

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or sync your usage data to any external servers. Your privacy is our priority, and all information remains under your control.

3.2 Data Security

  • Data is stored using Android's secure SharedPreferences and local storage mechanisms
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • Your usage statistics are only accessible within the app
  • You can clear all app data at any time through your device settings

3.3 Data Retention

Your usage data is retained on your device as long as the app is installed. Android's system typically retains usage statistics for 7-14 days. Historical data beyond this period may not be available due to Android system limitations.

4. What We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent about what we do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Personal messages, emails, or text content
  • ❌ Passwords or authentication credentials
  • ❌ Photos, videos, or media files
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contact information
  • ❌ Browsing history or web content
  • ❌ Financial information
  • ❌ Any content from within apps you use

5. Third-Party Services

ScreenTime does not integrate with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • You can view all your usage data within the app
  • You can modify or delete goals and app limits at any time
  • You can adjust notification preferences
  • You can revoke permissions at any time through Android Settings

6.2 Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

  • Uninstalling the app (this removes all local data)
  • Clearing app data through Android Settings > Apps > ScreenTime > Storage > Clear Data

6.3 Permission Management

You can revoke permissions at any time:

  • Usage Access: Settings > Apps > ScreenTime > App Permissions > Usage Access
  • Accessibility: Settings > Accessibility > Downloaded Apps > ScreenTime

Note: Revoking permissions may limit or disable certain features of the app.

7. Children's Privacy

ScreenTime is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted.

9. Compliance

9.1 Android System Requirements

ScreenTime complies with Android's privacy and security guidelines. We only request permissions that are necessary for the app's core functionality.

9.2 Data Protection

We follow best practices for data protection and privacy, ensuring that:

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Permissions are used only for their intended purpose
  • User data is not shared with third parties
